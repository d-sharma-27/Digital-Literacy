Stay Safe Online– Prevention Checklist

•	Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible 
•	Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank 
•	Always verify links, emails, and messages before clicking to avoid phishing scams 
•	Do not accept unknown payment requests or scan QR codes from untrusted sources (UPI safety) 
•	Check app permissions and only download apps from trusted sources like official app stores 
•	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions (financial safety) 
•	Regularly update your devices and apps to protect against security vulnerabilities 
•	Log out from accounts on shared or public devices after use 
•	Be cautious while sharing personal information on social media platforms 
•	In case of any cyber fraud, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930
